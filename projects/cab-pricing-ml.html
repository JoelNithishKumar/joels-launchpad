🚕 Uber & Lyft Cab Price Prediction

Random Forest Regression for Ride Fare Forecasting

<

📌 Project Summary

Developed a Random Forest regression model to predict ride fares for Uber and Lyft, achieving an R² = 0.98 and reducing RMSE to 1.44 (Lyft) and 2.10 (Uber).

⚙️ Tech Stack

  • Python, Pandas, scikit-learn, NumPy
  • Matplotlib/Seaborn for data exploration
  • Jupyter Notebook

📈 Results & Impact

  • R² score: 0.98
  • RMSE reduced from >5 to <2
  • Enabled accurate fare estimation and dynamic pricing simulation

📌 My Role

ML Engineer — handled EDA, feature engineering, model training/evaluation, and visualization for executive reporting.
